From western Philadelphia I hail where in my youth I'd play upon the green 'til -rue the day! - I found myself assail'd by ruffianec contemptible and mean. Although the spat was trivial and brief, it wounded my death mother deep within; and so, to giver her conscience sweet relief, she sent me forth to live amongst her kin. When to my port of call I'd been conveyed, I came upon a coachman most unique; and yet, I simply took the trip and paid, despite his cab's decor and fresh mystique. I survey all the land with princely mien in fair Bel-Air, where I do lay my scene.

Will Smith

One of the biggest names in Hollywood, born Willard Carroll Smith, Will Smith came to shine in The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air. The triple threat has garnered success in the music industry, the acting industry and the creative director industry. Born in 1968, Will smith decided at an early age that he would pursue Arts, despite his scholarship to MIT.

The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air turned Smith's career around. In 1990, just as he was to declare bankruptcy, NBC booked him for the Fresh Prince of Bel-Air. The Fresh-Prince is one of the most successful syndicated shows in television history.

As for his choice to pursue drama instead of science? The gamble paid off. As of 2014, his films have garnered over $6.8 billion at the Box office.
